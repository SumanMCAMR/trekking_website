  • overview

    An off the beaten path hike Nandikund and Ghiya Vinayak Pass for religious and seasoned hikers which connects the 2 sanctuaries Madhyamaheshwar and Kalpeshwar. The adventurous journey goes through the cover of Kachni Khal, the dramatic rolling grasslands, the high altitude lake Nandi Kund, finally crossing the Ghiya Vinayak pass at an altitude of 4991M, leading to Bansi Narayan, then descending to the last temple of Panch Kedar Kalpeshwar. This trek offers a varied landscape of spectacular views, dotted with Himalayan peaks like Chaukhambha, Mandani Parbat, Panpatia Snow field, Kedardome, Trishul, Neelkanth Peak - Known as Jewel of Gharwal, Parvati Peak, Nanda Devi, Dronagiri for to name a few from the top of Bansi Narayan and the Ghiya Vinayak pass.
