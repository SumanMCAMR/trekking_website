LEH: Visit the monasteries of Phyang and Spituk

Morning departure by private car with guide and visit of the Monasteries of Phyang and Spituk. Afternoon visit Leh Monastery, Sankar Gompa and Tsemo Castle. Night at the hotel.

LEH: Visit of the monasteries of Shey, Thiksey and Hemis

Morning departure by private car with guide and visit of the Royal Fortress of Shey, built on the crest of a mountain as well as the monastery of Thikse which overlooks the valley and has a very large statue of the Buddha. In the afternoon, visit the Monastery of Hemis, the most important monastery in Ladakh, built in 1605, it brings together more than 500 monks. It houses a unique collection of small paintings retracing the life of the great masters of Lamaism.


Visit of the Royal Fortress of Shey

Leh > Likir > Yangtang

Morning after breakfast departure by jeep for Likir (52kms) visit of the monastery and departure of the trek for Yangtang. Departure through arid terrain, climb to the small pass of Pobe la. Arrival at the village of Sumdo, Crossing the river and continuation for the Charatse la pass. Half an hour downhill from Charatse la to reach Yangtang. Night with resident.

Yangtang > Rizong > Yangtang

Morning after breakfast departure to visit the Rizong Monastery, a rocky fortress perched on the mountainside in a grandiose setting, it was founded in the 19th century. Visit of the main temples and return to Yangtang. Night with resident.


5 hour of Walking

Yangtang - Ang (about 4 - 5 hours)

Depart Yangtang for Ang. By Sermanchan La and Meptek La. On the way visit the monastery of Hemis Shukpachan. Night with resident.

Ang > Khalse > Uleytokpo

Morning after breakfast, departure for Khalse passing the village of Themisgang, one of the richest villages in Ladakh, you can see apricot trees, apple trees, walnut trees and beautiful houses and the monastery. An hour's walk to the Bongbong La pass and downhill to reach the national highway and then departure by jeep for Uleytokpo. Night at the hotel.


5-6 hour Trekking

Leh > Nubra Valley via Khardung La

Early in the morning departure for the Nubra Valley by Khardong La (5540m) the highest road in the world. 3 hours by jeep to reach Tirith after Khardung-la. Installation at the camp. Afternoon visit Summur, Kyagar and hot spring. Overnight in tent or hotel.
